Demo (This demo shows the tampering of IEEE datasets)
A parameter K is taken in order to check the count of tampered patches. For example if K is 50 and the tampered patches exceed the value of 50 then the image is labeled as tampered. To decide the value of K , one needs to break down image into patches first and needs top observe how many of the total patches are tampered in a typical dataset.
